Supervisor.skill is an end-user app for people who lead, coach, or support a team. It helps you organize tasks, track follow-ups, and keep your notes in one place. It is built for Windows and is meant to be simple to run.

This project follows the idea behind colleague-skill and ex-skill, with a focus on the work of a supervisor or mentor. It gives you a clear way to manage team support without extra setup.
